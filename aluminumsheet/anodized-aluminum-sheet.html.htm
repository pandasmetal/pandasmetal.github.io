The advantage of Anodized Aluminum Sheet

1. Good processability
Anodized Aluminum Sheet is decorative strong, moderate hardness, it can bend easily, suitable for continuous high-speed stamping, convenient to process into products directly, no need complicated surface treatment, greatly reduce the production cycle and production cost.
2. Good weathering resistance
The standard anodizing layer 3μm of Anodized Aluminum Sheett for the indoor long time use, is not discolored, non-corroding, not oxidated and no rust. With thick anodized layer 10μm of anodized aluminium sheet, suitable for outdoor use, it is not discolored during expose to the sunlight.
3. Strong metal feeling
After anodizing, the aluminium surface become high hardness, up to gem grade, good scratch resistance. There is no paint cover, preserve the metallic color of the aluminium sheet, highling the modern metallic feel, improve product level and added value.
4. High fire retardancy
Pure metal products, there is no paint and no any chemicals on the surface, not burning at 600℃ high temperature, no poisonous gases are produced, meet environmental requirements.
5. Strong fouling resistance
No gingerprints, no blemishes and marks, easy to clean, no corrosion spots are produced.
6. Good applicability
Wide-range use, suitable for metal ceiling, curtain wall plate, aluminum composite panel, fire-proof plate, aluminium honeycomb board, aluminium panel, electrical panel, cabinet panel, furniture panel, etc.
