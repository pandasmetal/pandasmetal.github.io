Marine Aluminium Plate

Common alloy : 5052/5454/5754 5086/5083/3003

Application parts : Deck, engine pedestal, ship side, bottom outer plate and other parts of yacht, grain carrier, fishing boat, work boat, sand carrier and other ships.

Advantages : The marine aluminum plate can reduce the weight of the ship, increase the speed, reduce fuel consumption, improve the aspect ratio of the ship, increase stability, have good anti-corrosion ability, and increase the service life of the ship.

Oil Tanker Aluminum Plate

Common alloy : 5082/5052/5754/5454/5182

Application parts : aluminum alloy tank car body, coaming, wave plate, tank mouth, etc.

Advantages : The body will not rust when scratched, durable appearance, easy to clean and long service life. Aluminum alloy has low density and high strength. The light body reduces fuel consumption and tire wear during transportation, thus reducing daily operation and maintenance costs.

Automotive Aluminum Plate

Common alloy : 5182/6061/5083/6111/5052/5754

Application parts : door, engine hood, automobile heat shield, automobile hub, bus skin, bus trunk, bus door, etc.

Advantages : The automotive aluminum plate has obvious weight reduction and energy saving effects, and the naturally formed oxide film on its surface has good corrosion resistance and long service life. The body is light, the center of the body is lowered, and the driving is more stable, comfortable and safe. Environmental protection, high recovery rate.

Details

Aerospace Aluminum Plate

Common alloy: 2024/2A12/5A06/7075/7A04/7A09

Application parts : aircraft skin, wing, aircraft mailbox, aircraft stringer, spacer frame, damping strut, weldment, aircraft wall, etc.

Advantages : low aluminum alloy density reduces the overall weight of the aircraft fuselage, thus reducing the energy consumption of the aircraft. Aluminum alloy flying in high altitude atmosphere can reduce atmospheric corrosion and prolong aircraft life to a certain extent. Durable, economical and environmentally friendly, it is an excellent aluminum for aerospace.
