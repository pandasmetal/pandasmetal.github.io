Stucco embossed aluminum sheets are a thin (.024") aluminum sheet with a wide variety of uses and applications. The embossing process reduces the light reflectivity of the metal.

Application Instruction

stucco embossed aluminum coil are usually used in refrigerators, air conditioners and refrigeration equipment, and pipe insulation, the products have good thermal conductivity, heat dissipation due to the special nature of the pattern. Such mateirals are widely used in the fields of refregerator, wine cabinet, solar panel, decorative aluminium product, linghting fixture, lighting box, sanitizer, kitchen cabinet and central air conditioning system.At present, in the refrigeration industry, the 3003 series of rust-proof aluminum coil are widely used; In pipe insulation and packaging 1000 series are widely used.

our stucco embossed aluminum coil provides a white primer or silver (mill finish) surface that is easy to clean. Either side is useable, and the pattern looks the same. The white-painted side has slight but consistent imperfections and does not have a PVC coating. stucco embossed aluminum coil is a great low maintenance wainscoting.
