Product Description
Orange Peel Anodized Aluminum Coil  has an oxide layer with thickness 3-20um after anodized treatment. It can be customized with different alloy, temper, specifications, weight and packing. The longer the processing time, the aluminum oxide layer is thicker, the surface color more white, the price higher, the higher end market.

The oxide layer 3um and below, mainly used in waterproofing, such as air-conditioner, refrigerator,etc; 5-6um, mainly in surface process, used in spray, color coated process, such as licence plate, curtain wall, interior decoration, signage, advertising board,etc; 6-10um, due to its high strength and corrosion resistance, is widely used, mainly in machine shell, lighting, consumer electronics, crafts, household appliances, interior decoration,  furniture, automotive decoration and other industries; 20um, ship building, aircraft, electrical components,etc.
