1000 series pure aluminum disc circle has an aluminum content of not less than 99.0%, also known as 1000 series pure aluminum. According to the different purity, it can be divided into high purity aluminum and industrial pure aluminum. Aluminum with not less than 99.85% aluminum content is high purity aluminum, while 99.0% ~ 99.85% (excluding 99.85%) aluminum content is industrial pure aluminum. The alloy has the characteristics of low density, good electrical conductivity, high thermal conductivity, high latent heat of melting, large light reflection coefficient, small thermal neutron absorption cross-sectional area, and beautiful appearance. The surface of aluminum in air can form a dense solid oxide film, which can prevent the invasion of oxygen, and therefore has good corrosion resistance.
