1060 Aluminum alloy, with an aluminum content of more than 99.6%, is also known as pure aluminum. It belongs to a common series in the aluminum alloy family. It is industrial pure aluminum, with high plasticity, corrosion resistance, conductivity and thermal conductivity, but low strength, unable to be strengthened by heat treatment, and poor machinability; It can be gas welding, hydrogen atom welding and contact welding, and is not easy to braze; Easy to bear all kinds of pressure processing, drawing and bending.

According to the product type, it can be divided into

  • 1060 metal pure aluminum sheet plate
  • 1060 metal pure aluminum coil
  • 1060 metal pure aluminum strip
  • 1060 metal pure aluminum circle

According to the surface treatment, it can be divided into

  • Color 1060 aluminum
  • Embossed 1060 aluminum
  • Anodized 1060 aluminum
  • Mirror 1060 aluminum
